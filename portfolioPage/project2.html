You-Fit

Developed a fitness application with android studio. Google Firebase was employed for user authentication and can handle 200,000 users concurrently. Application enables users to log in, set up exercises and diet, and tracking features to track user progress.

Introduction

With more and more things becoming virtual, people are benefiting from its asynchronous nature. It saves time and everything can be tracked. Health and Fitness is such an area. It's absolutely necessary for people to keep track of their health habits, exercise routines and diet on the go daily. Our application will provide people to keep track of their diet, stream exercise videos to learn asynchronously to avoid dedicating a fixed time to go to fitness centers. Each user can create an account with a unique username and a password. They can stream exercise videos and log their activities to keep a track.

Features

  • Option to register and create an account.
  • Sign into individual account with password.
  • Watch posted training videos anytime.
  • Nutritional Videos with the ideal diet plans for individual fitness goals.
  • Users' consistency(or attendance) is checked with each sign in.
  • A simple timer to keep track of activity time.
